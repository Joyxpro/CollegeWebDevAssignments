The Taj Mahal

Explore one of the world's most iconic structures.

About the Site

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is a stunning mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Completed in 1653, it stands as one of the most iconic symbols of love in the world. The white marble structure, with intricate inlay work and beautiful gardens, attracts millions of visitors every year.

Things to Know Before You Visit

  • Best time to visit: Sunrise or sunset for breathtaking views.
  • Nearby attractions: Agra Fort, Mehtab Bagh.
  • Don't miss: The intricate marble inlay and the reflection pool.
