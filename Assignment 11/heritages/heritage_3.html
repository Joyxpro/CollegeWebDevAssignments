Jantar Mantar

Discover the ancient astronomical observatory in Jaipur.

About the Site

Jantar Mantar in Jaipur is an 18th-century astronomical observatory built by Maharaja Jai Singh II. It features impressive instruments used for measuring time, predicting eclipses, and tracking celestial bodies. The observatory is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its architectural precision and scientific significance.

Things to Know Before You Visit

  • Best time to visit: Early morning or late afternoon.
  • Nearby attractions: City Palace, Hawa Mahal.
  • Don't miss: The massive sundials and astronomical instruments.
