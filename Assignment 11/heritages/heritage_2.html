India Gate

A monumental war memorial dedicated to Indian soldiers.

About the Site

India Gate is a war memorial located in New Delhi, India. Built in 1931, the monument honors the soldiers who died in World War I. The archway, standing at 42 meters, is inscribed with the names of more than 13,000 soldiers and is a prominent landmark and popular tourist attraction.

Things to Know Before You Visit

  • Best time to visit: Early morning or late evening to avoid crowds.
  • Nearby attractions: Rashtrapati Bhavan, Connaught Place.
  • Don't miss: The beautiful evening illumination of the monument.
